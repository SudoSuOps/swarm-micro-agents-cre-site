Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

This policy explains what data Caballerz Network LLC DBA Swarm & Bee ("Swarm & Bee", "we") collects when you use swarmandbee.ai and related services, how we use it, and the controls you have.

1. What we collect

  • Contact submissions. When you send a message via the form at /#contact we receive your name, email, company (optional), and message. We use this to respond to your inquiry.
  • Server logs. Our edge provider (Cloudflare) records standard request metadata — IP, user agent, timestamp, path — for abuse prevention and performance. Retention follows Cloudflare's defaults.
  • No analytics cookies. We do not set advertising or analytics cookies. We do not use third-party trackers.

2. How we use it

  • To respond to inbound inquiries.
  • To operate and secure the site.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. How we store it

Inbound messages are delivered via Resend (transactional email) and, optionally, a private Discord channel. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers. We retain contact submissions for as long as reasonably needed to respond and fulfill the inquiry.

4. Your rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of any personal data we hold by emailing build@swarmandbee.ai. California residents have specific rights under the CCPA; EEA/UK residents have rights under GDPR. We will honor verifiable requests within statutory timeframes.

5. Children

The site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from them.

6. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
